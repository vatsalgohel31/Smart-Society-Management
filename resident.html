Q: How do I approve a guest entry?

A: Use the "Approve Guest" feature to generate a unique PIN code for your guest. Share this PIN with your guest to allow entry.

Q: What is the process for raising a complaint?

A: Click on "Raise Complaint", provide a title and detailed description, then submit. You can track status in "My Complaints".

Q: When is the maintenance due date?

A: Maintenance is due by the 5th of every month. Late payments may incur penalties.

Q: How can I book common facilities?

A: Contact the admin office at least 48 hours in advance to book facilities like the clubhouse or party hall.

Q: What are the visitor parking rules?

A: Visitors can use designated visitor parking spots. No parking in resident spots. Maximum 4 hours parking allowed.
